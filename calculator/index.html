Why a range?

Every day is different. You'll move more (burn more calories) on some days and move less (burn less calories) on other days. This can also affect how hungry you are day to day.

This range gives you more freedom and flexibility when working towards your goal.

If you want individual advice on your ideal calorie intake, consult a doctor or dietitian.
